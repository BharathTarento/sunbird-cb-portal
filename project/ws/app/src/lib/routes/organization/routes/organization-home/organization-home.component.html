In-Service Training Programme (IST)

The Training Division, DoPT sponsors the ‘In-Service Training Programme’ in various ATIs/CTIs/Other Premier Training Institutes by inviting proposals from them on relevant themes for Officers of All India Service (IAS, IPS & IFoS), officers working under the Central Staffing Scheme, officers of Central Secretariat Service (CSS) and officers of Central Secretariat Stenographer Service (CSSS) (DS/Sr. PPS and above level).   These programmes provide select middle to higher management level officers an opportunity to update their skills in area of their own choice—in a sector they are currently working in, or in an area they expect to work in near future, or in a theme they feel they should specialize. IST covers broad thematic areas: such as governance issues; sector specific coverage of agriculture, education, rural development, urban development, social policy; climate change, Natural Resource Management; ethics, corruption; e-governance; fiscal policy and macroeconomic management, WTO and trade issues; PPP, Project Appraisal, Environment Impact Assessment, Project Analysis; Public Policy making, Decision science and quantitative methods, leadership, ethics, gender, smart cities, etc.  

Eligibility:

  • Officers fulfilling the following condition are eligible to apply for In-service Training
  • AIS (IAS, IPS IFoS) officers with minimum 4 years of service (as on April 1, 2023) and up to 2018 batch
  • Officers working in the Central Staffing Scheme under the Govt. of India (with minimum of 4 years of Service upto the rank of Secretary to the Govt. of India and Chief Secretary in the State Govt. and equivalent)
  • Officers of Central Secretariat Service (CSS) and Central Secretariat Stenographer Service (CSSS) (Officers of the level of DS/Sr. PPS and above)
  • An Officer can attend only one In-service training programme during an IST calendar
  • The officers should not have attended the same training from the same institute in last 3 years
